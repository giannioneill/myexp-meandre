An error in myExperiment has occured.

Please help us fix this problem, by doing one of the following two options.

Report a bug online

Take a few seconds and report a bug directly to our bug tracking system. This is the quickest way to report a bug.

Report a bug by email

Send an email to bugs@myexperiment.org.

What shall I put in my bug report?

Tell us as much as you can about what you were doing when the error occured. Also, please tell us which operating system (ie. Windows XP, Mac OS X, GNU/Linux, etc) and which web browser (ie. Firefox, Internet Explorer 7, etc) you use.

You can even attach a screenshot. How do I do this in Windows?, How do I do this on a Mac?, How do I do this on GNU/Linux?.
