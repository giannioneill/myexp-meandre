
Quick notes for installation from the repository:

  (1) copy config/database.yml.pre to config/database.yml

  (2) copy config/environment_private.rb.pre to config/environment_private.rb

  (3) copy vendor/plugins/sitealizer/lib/config.yml.pre to
      vendor/plugins/sitealizer/lib/config.yml

These files contain passwords for MySQL, SMTP and Site statistics respectively.
